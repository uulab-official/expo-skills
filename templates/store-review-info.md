## Platform Review Checklist

### Apple

- App Review contact is complete.
- Demo account works.
- Privacy policy URL is present and public.
- App Privacy answers match actual data collection.
- Required permissions have clear in-app usage descriptions.
- Review notes explain login and special setup.
- Screenshots match current app UI.

### Google Play

- App access instructions are complete.
- Privacy policy URL is present and public.
- Data safety form matches actual data collection/sharing.
- Ads declaration is correct.
- Content rating questionnaire is ready.
- Target audience assumptions are documented.
- Permission declarations match app behavior.
- Internal/closed testing track is ready when required.
